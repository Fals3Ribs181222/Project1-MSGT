In My Own Words

I bring over 22 years of accountancy teaching experience spanning both State and ISC boards. My career reached its apex as Head of Department – Accountancy at Jamnabai Narsee School, one of Mumbai's most prestigious institutions, where I led the department from 2019 to 2025 — overseeing curriculum, examinations, and a team of fellow teachers.

What sets me apart is results: under my guidance, the class average climbed from 75% to 97%. I've mentored thousands of students through some of the most challenging accountancy topics — partnership accounts, company accounts, cash flow analysis, and ratio interpretation — turning abstract concepts into second nature.

Beyond the classroom, I have served as an External Examiner for the CISCE Council since 2013, and was appointed as a Resource Person for the Council Account Cell Meeting in both 2015 and 2025 — a recognition of my peer-level expertise within the ISC examination body.

At my Study Circle, every student benefits from this depth of institutional knowledge, exam-pattern insight, and a teaching style I've refined over two decades in front of Mumbai's most driven students.
